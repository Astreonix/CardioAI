Process

How CardioAI Works

From raw clinical values to an interpretable risk verdict in three steps.

01

Enter Clinical Data

Input 13 standard diagnostic values — age, cholesterol, blood pressure, ECG readings, and more — via the assessment form.

02

ML Inference

A trained Random Forest model (100 trees, depth 5) processes your scaled feature vector and outputs a calibrated probability score.

03

Risk Report

Receive a risk tier (Low / Moderate / High), probability percentage, and the specific clinical flags driving the assessment.

04

SHAP Explainability

Every prediction is interpretable. SHAP values reveal which features pushed the risk score up or down for that specific patient.
